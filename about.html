hi there! i am kael, a front-end developer and ui/ux designer who enjoys creating minimal and intuitive digital experiences. i have a strong eye for design and i love finding and working with cool, fun, and minimal fonts!

i approach both design and code with curiosity and care, enjoying the process of turning ideas into simple, functional interfaces. while my confidence sometimes fluctuates between “this works well!” and “is this even working???” i learned to value this process through experimentation, refining details, and improving with every project.

beyond technical skills, i value empathy, patience, and clear communication. i enjoy thinking from the user’s perspective, collaborating thoughtfully, and building at my own pace—focusing more on growth and the process than on chasing perfection.
